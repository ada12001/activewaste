Website Redesign Proposal

A strategic redesign for Active Waste Solutions to modernize the website, improve user experience, and strengthen long-term performance while protecting search visibility.

Prepared for: Active Waste Solutions

Prepared by: LuxWeb

Estimated Timeline: 4 to 6 weeks

Project Overview

LuxWeb proposes a complete website redesign built in a fresh WordPress environment using Elementor. This approach improves flexibility, simplifies content management, and creates a scalable platform while preserving existing functionality and SEO value.

Objectives

  • Deliver a modern, professional design aligned with business goals
  • Improve mobile responsiveness and overall user experience
  • Maintain existing SEO value and rankings
  • Preserve functionality while enhancing performance
  • Provide a scalable platform for future growth

Timeline

Estimated timeline: 4 to 6 weeks from project start, depending on feedback turnaround and content approvals.

Scope of Work

  • 1. Discovery & Brand Direction If formal brand guidelines are unavailable, we establish visual foundations including color palette, typography, and style direction.
  • 2. Design & Development Custom homepage and core page redesign, full content migration, fresh WordPress installation, Elementor setup, and mobile/tablet optimization.
  • 3. SEO Preservation URL preservation where possible, 301 redirects, metadata migration, sitemap submission, and technical SEO best practices.
  • 4. Functionality & Integrations Migration of forms/contact workflows, integration of existing third-party tools, and implementation of approved new features.
  • 5. Quality Assurance & Launch Cross-browser and device testing, optimization, final review, approval, and live deployment.
